The Design Thinking
Portfolio Gallery

This gallery showcases outcomes from a semester-long Design Thinking course for fourth-semester B.Tech students at Mahindra University.

The course develops analytical rigor, systems thinking, and structured problem framing. Students work on real-world challenges and produce conceptually robust, well-justified design proposals grounded in research and clear decision logic.

These projects reflect disciplined thinking as much as creative intent.
